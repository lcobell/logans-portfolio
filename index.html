About Me

My name is Logan CoBell and I've been working in the restaurant industry since 2011. I began my restaurant journey in Charleston, SC working at the Market Pavilion Hotel's Roof Top Bar and Grill 225 as well as Charleston staples S.N.O.B., The Charleston Grill, and Stars. In 2015 I moved to St. Thomas, USVI and began my transition to bartending at the Cruzan Beach Club and later at the islands highest volume bar, Tap and Still where I was a manager and head bartender for two and a half years. Upon moving back to the mainland during quarantine I was one of the opening bartenders at Orion's Roof (recently named to Parade Magazine, Open Table, and Bumble's top 100 Date-Night Restaurants in America for 2021) where I worked for 15 months, and was the longest tenured bartender. In December of 2021, I decided that I was ready to take the next step in my professional development and move to Chicago and pursue a position with Lettuce Entertain You Enterprises' RPM Steak, one of the top steakhouses in the country while also studying for a future in Software Development. Ultimately I would like to use my experience in some of the countries finest restaurants and bars to design software applications to improve the restaurant industry. ❤️
